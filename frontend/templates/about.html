About BizFindr

Empowering businesses and researchers with comprehensive Connecticut business registration data

What We Do

BizFindr is a powerful platform that provides easy access to Connecticut business registration data. Our mission is to make business information more accessible and useful for entrepreneurs, researchers, and the general public.

Comprehensive Data

Access detailed business registration information from the Connecticut Secretary of State's office.

Advanced Search

Powerful search capabilities to find exactly what you're looking for with filters and keywords.

Insightful Analytics

Visualize business trends and statistics to gain valuable market insights.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, BizFindr was created to solve a simple problem: business registration data should be easily accessible to everyone who needs it.

What started as a small project to make Connecticut business data more accessible has grown into a comprehensive platform used by entrepreneurs, researchers, and business professionals across the state.

Our team is committed to providing accurate, up-to-date information and continuously improving our platform to better serve our users.
